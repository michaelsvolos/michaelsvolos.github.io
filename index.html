Michael Svolos is a senior at Stanford University majoring in Music with a concentration in Music, Science, and Technology. He is minoring in Computer Science. He is an instrumentalist and arranger, performing on flute, alto saxophone, and trombone. He is also a composer, making computer music that often involves writing code as well as more traditional tools like digital audio workstations. He is interested in all things audio and technology, especially when both are involved.
